Data Dictionary:

ID -- The policy ID (used to link with the claims dataset)
DriverAge -- The driver age, in years
VehiclePower -- The power of the car 
VehicleAge -- The vehicle age, in years 
VehicleBrand -- The car brand (dummy values) 
FuelType -- The car fuel type: Diesel or regular (Petrol)
Density -- The population density of the city the owner of the car lives in
Region -- The policy regions in ‘Country Y’ (dummy values) 
Area -- The area code
Exposure -- The exposure period
BM -- Bonus/malus, between 50 and 350: 100 means malus in ‘Country Y’.
ClaimNum -- Number of claims during the exposure period. 

Note: This dataset contains dummy values and shouldn’t be linked to real world.
